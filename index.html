Peter Stastny

Frontend developer & designer

Hi! I am Peter Stastny, a multimedia design student focusing on frontend web development and UI design based In Copenhagen.

I am passionate about designing and building websites that are user-friendly, accessible and stand-out with their attention to detail.

Some of my skills include working in graphic and wireframing applications, HTML + CSS + JavaScript and photography.

I am always eager to learn and refine my skills. Feel free to contact me with any questions or take a look at my past projects.
